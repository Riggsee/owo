  • About Us

  • Create
About Us

Other World Online (OwO) was founded with one goal in mind. To gather gamers together and produce an environment where no matter what your game or playstyle is you will be able to find a group, create a team, or produce content to a group of likeminded individuals.

Our founding members come from very diverse environments whether it be roleplay, streaming, esports, or almost anything else you can ask questions, find help, or even just share your stories to the rest of the community. The goal of OwO is to have a light-hearted community where all the members can make friends, play, and promote whatever game they choose. This community also contains no real regulations, activity requirements, or rules surrounding it. We offer our community

  • A fun and welcoming atmosphere.
  • Promotion for your channel or livestream.
  • A full merchandise shop for community created art and images.
Our Community

Our community bases itself on the social aspect of mulitplayer gaming and pride ourselves on diversity. Our Discord features multiple text and chat rooms and the admins are always looking for new groups to accept no matter what games you play. With an incredibly active staff you'll be able to have any requests or concerns heard and responded to in a timely manner so you can get back into the game faster.

While our main form of community interaction is through our Discord server we have many more features and additions planned as the community grows.

Create

Other World Online is always looking for artistic talent. Whether you are a content creator that thrives in a creative environment, or you want to design art for the community we will always look into your art work. In the future we aim to have all of our community created art featured on various apparel, posters, and whatever else we can print it on!
